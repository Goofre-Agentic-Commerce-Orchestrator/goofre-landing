The Agentic Era is Leaving SMBs and Developers Behind

The SMB Crisis

Agentic commerce (one-click purchasing) is the new standard, but Canadian SMBs are locked out. They lack the tech stack to navigate the complex Google commerce centric stack.

The Protocol Bottleneck

Google’s Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) is the best framework, but it is siloed and too heavy for a local merchant to deploy.

The Developer Dilemma

As legacy e-commerce platforms automate, thousands of freelance ecom-developers are watching their traditional skill sets (store design) evaporate.

The Goofre Orchestration Ecosystem

A three-tier architecture turning complexity into voice-activated simplicity.

1. The Engine (Goofre OS)
Open-source middleware translating the heavy Google UCP stack (GMC, Analytics, Search Console, Ads).
2. The Interface (Goofre App) Hack Canada 2026 Build
A voice-activated "Bento" Command Center. Distills deep AI insights into a 4-quadrant interface.
3. The Orchestrator
Empowering displaced developers to manage the Goofre OS for merchants, turning them into essential AI operators.
